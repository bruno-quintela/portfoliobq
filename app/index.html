Who am I
I'm a Web Developer and Computer Graphics enthusiast. In the past months i've decided to put together my passion for CG with my current developer skills. This website is the result of that.
What I Do
My current focus is to create state of the art visuals and experiences for the web. To achieve perfect balance between visual delivery and performance is always a goal, but this website is targeting medium-high end specs.
What's Next
VR is coming and i'm looking forward to it! For the first time in the past decades we will have the opportunity to change how we surf the web. So my future wishes are to be in a project related to that, as a web developer or creative director.
