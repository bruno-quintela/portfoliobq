Who am I
I'm a web developer who recently got into computer graphics. I love music, videogames (MMORPG and JRPG addicted), and digital art, specially lowpoly, procedural and generative.
My current focus isto create state of the art visuals for the web. To achieve a perfect ballance between visual delivery and performance is allways my pripority.
In the next five years we will enter into the age of Virtual Reality, and that will have a massive impact in the way you surf the web, so i expect to be in a vanguard project related to that, as a web developer or creative director.
